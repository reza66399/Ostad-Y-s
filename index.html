About Us

My name is Zahra Babakhani, a proud graduate in Mathematics from the University of Shiraz, with over seven years of experience teaching YÖS content to students. Throughout my journey, I've had the privilege of working with many bright and hardworking students, many of whom come from low and middle-income backgrounds.

I've noticed that despite their talent and dedication, these students are often underrepresented at top universities, facing significant financial challenges that force them to give up on their dreams. This realization inspired me to take action.

To address this issue, I founded this platform to provide accessible, high-quality education to students in Iran and Farsi-speaking students around the world. Our goal is to empower you to reach your full potential and gain the opportunities you deserve, regardless of your financial circumstances.

We are here to support you every step of the way, and I sincerely wish you all success in your academic endeavors.

Our Services

We offer a variety of services tailored to students who wish to pursue higher education in Turkey, including:

  • Yös Exam Preparation: Expert guidance and resources to help you excel in the YÖS exams, ensuring you are fully prepared to meet the academic challenges ahead.
  • University Application Assistance: Step-by-step support through the application process, from selecting the right programs to submitting your applications.
  • Accommodation Assistance: Helping you find safe, comfortable, and affordable accommodation that suits your needs and preferences during your studies in Turkey.
  • Scholarship Guidance: Assistance in identifying and applying for scholarships to help reduce the financial burden of studying abroad.
  • Visa and Immigration Support: Navigating the visa application and immigration process to ensure a smooth transition to your new academic journey.
  • Cultural Orientation: Providing insights and resources to help you adapt to the cultural and social environment in Turkey, making your transition easier and more enjoyable.
